Privacy Policy
Last updated: February 21, 2026

Summary: Subcis collects minimal data. We only store your chosen display name and gender preference locally on your device. Voice data is transmitted in real-time but never recorded or stored.
1. Information We Collect
Information you provide:

Display Name: The name you choose to show other participants (stored locally on your device only)
Gender Selection: Used to match you with appropriate circles (stored locally on your device only)
Information collected automatically:

Anonymous User ID: A random identifier generated on your device to manage sessions (not linked to your identity)
Session Data: Temporary connection data required for voice communication

2. Voice Data
Subcis enables real-time voice communication for Quran recitation circles. Important points:

Voice is transmitted in real-time to other participants only
We do NOT record or store any voice data
Voice data is not used for any purpose other than live transmission
When you leave a session, your voice data is not retained

3. Data Storage
All personal preferences (name, gender) are stored locally on your device only. We do not maintain user accounts or store personal information on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:

LiveKit: For real-time voice communication. LiveKit processes voice data in transit but does not store recordings. See LiveKit's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

As required by law
To protect our rights or safety

6. Children's Privacy
Subcis is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app only stores a display name chosen by the user.
